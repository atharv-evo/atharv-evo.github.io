Government Agro Loan Schemes

Kisan Credit Card (KCC)

Short-term credit for crop production with low interest rates.

  • Low interest rates for farmers
  • Flexible repayment options
  • Covers crop production expenses
  • Insurance coverage available
Learn More

PM Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana

Direct income support for small and marginal farmers.

  • ₹6,000 per year in three installments
  • Direct transfer to bank accounts
  • No middlemen involved
  • Simple online application
Learn More

NABARD Agriculture Loan Scheme

Long-term finance for farm development and irrigation.

  • Long repayment periods
  • Subsidized interest rates
  • Modern farming equipment financing
  • Irrigation system support

About Us

This website is created to assist farmers in understanding and accessing agricultural loan schemes with ease. We aim to simplify the process and empower India's farming community.

Our mission is to bridge the information gap between government schemes and farmers, ensuring that every farmer can benefit from the financial support available to them.

We believe that by providing clear, accessible information and easy eligibility checking, we can contribute to the prosperity of our agricultural sector.
